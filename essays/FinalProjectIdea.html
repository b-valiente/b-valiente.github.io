Final Project Idea

01 Nov 2022

Overview

The problem: Being healthy is often an overlooked aspect when in college and going to the gym is the the last thought on peoples minds. However, sometimes you just need the right group of people or a partner to help get you started in the gym or to help you stay consistent.

The Solution: My Solution to this problem would be to create a website called "Better Together" where students can connect with each other and find partners or groups to workout with.

Approach

When the user creates an account and logs in they will be able to see their profile which will include their personal info such as name, age etc along with their workout preferences and partner preferences. Mockup Page Ideas:
  • Landing Page
  • Profile Page
  • Workout Preference Selector
  • Workout Schedule Selector
  • Partner Preference Selector

Use Case Ideas

  • Landing page allows users to create an account, login and logout. Their Profile page will show their workout schedule, partner preference and workout preference. May include a calendar to track progress.
  • Preference selectors will allow user to customize what kind of workout they want to do, which gender they prefer to workout with, and their schedule that they want to work with.

Beyond The Basics

  • Allow user to select skill level
  • Message back and forth between potential partners/groups
  • Achievements and rewards for being consistent
